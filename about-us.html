About Us

About South African Wheelchairs

Our story

Founded on the principles of compassion, innovation, and accessibility, our company aims to bridge the gap in wheelchair provision in South Africa. Our team of experts, including healthcare professionals, engineers, and advocates for disability rights, collaborate to design and manufacture wheelchairs that meet the unique needs of our users.

Our mission

At South African Wheelchairs, we're passionate about creating a more inclusive and accessible world. Join us on our journey to empower individuals with mobility impairments and help us make a difference in the lives of those we serve.

 
Accessibility

We believe that mobility is a fundamental human right. Our goal is to make high-quality wheelchairs accessible to all who need them.

 
Innovation

We're committed to staying at the forefront of wheelchair technology, continuously improving our designs and materials to enhance user experience.

 
Empowerment

Our wheelchairs are designed to empower users to take control of their lives, pursue their passions, and live without limits.

 
Compassion

We understand the challenges faced by individuals with mobility impairments and their families. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional support and service.
