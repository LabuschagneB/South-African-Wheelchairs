Welcome to South African Wheelchairs

At South African Wheelchairs, we're dedicated to enhancing the lives of individuals with mobility impairments. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable wheelchairs that empower users to live independently, confidently, and without limits.

Our Mission

At South African Wheelchairs, we're passionate about creating a more inclusive and accessible world. Join us on our journey to empower individuals with mobility impairments and help us make a difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our Story

Founded on the principles of compassion, innovation, and accessibility, our company aims to bridge the gap in wheelchair provision in South Africa. Our team of experts, including healthcare professionals, engineers, and advocates for disability rights, collaborate to design and manufacture wheelchairs that meet the unique needs of our users.

Our Products

Manual Wheelchairs

A Manual Wheelchair is a wheeled mobility chair that allows a person with limited mobility to become mobile and independent again. These wheelchairs can either be propelled by the person in the wheelchair or be pushed by a carer.

View All

Powered Wheelchairs

A motorized wheelchair, powerchair, electric wheelchair, or electric-powered wheelchair (EPW) is a wheelchair that is propelled by means of an electric motor (usually using differential steering) rather than manual power.

View All

Scooters

A mobility scooter is an electric personal transporter used as mobility aid for people with physical impairment, mostly auxiliary to a powered wheelchair but configured like a motorscooter.

View All

Walking Aid

Walking aids are devices designed to assist individuals with mobility impairments in maintaining upright ambulation. These aids include assistive canes, crutches, walkers, and more specialized devices.

View All

Bathroom Aid

Choosing the right bath aids is essential to ensure safety, comfort, and independence for those who have limited mobility.

View All

Hoist & Slings

These are devices designed to lift and transfer individuals with limited mobility from one place to another, such as from a bed to a wheelchair.

View All

Pressure Care

Bad posture in a wheelchair or bed puts pressure on your skin and increases the risk of pressure ulcers. Changing positions relieves your body of pressure and ensures blood circulates to the skin.

View All

Respiration Aid

Respiratory support on a wheelchair is more than just a hose to your mouth. It is a total system with various components, such as the breathing unit, suction equipment and the connection to your wheelchair.

View All

Kiddies Range

Pediatric wheelchairs are durable and versatile, giving children with disabilities the freedom to partake in any childhood activity as much as an able-bodied child, so you can make the most informed decision for your child.
